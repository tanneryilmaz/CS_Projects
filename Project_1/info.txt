NOTE: All percentages should be entered as values less than 1. For example, 
7% should be entered as 0.07, not as 7.

Currently, the program crashes if you run the simulation for more than a certain number of years. Code will be added to fix this. 

Input file format:
1. simulation length in years 
2. whether or not the person is financially literate (0 if not, 1 if yes)
3. starting credit card and student loan debt
4. monthly payment percentage for credit card and student loan debt
5. monthly additional payment amount on credit card debt
6. price of house the person wants to purchase
7. percentage down payment the person will make on the house
8. yearly interest rate on mortgage loan
9. yearly interest paid to person in their savings account
10. monthly cost of renting an apartment
11. person's yearly salary
12. number of years person will take to pay of their mortgage
